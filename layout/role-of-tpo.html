ROLE OF TPO CELL

TIEM-P has been consistently topping, during the last eight years in the list of campus placement records among private institutions in West Bengal. Students of TIEM-P are highly regarded by employers from industry and the public sector. Facilitating students by way of offering information, advice, guidance and job-seeking support for students is considered as a primary responsibility at TIEM-P. Career Advisors are available to discuss ideas & plans, and students are encouraged to attend the wide range of talks, information fairs and recruitment events.

With this goal in mind, the Training & Placement Office (TPO) Cell:

  • Arranges for in-plant industrial training for students during their summer and Intra semester break.
  • Encourages students to become entrepreneurs, through Entrepreneurship Development Programmes.
  • Training facilities for students to face competitive exams like JELET and prepares them for the interviews.
  • Promotes career guidance & counseling through lectures by senior corporate executives and visiting professors.
  • Organizing campus interviews for pre-final and final year students with industrial and business houses of repute from all over India.
